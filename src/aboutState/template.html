About Us

Surf Garage opened in May of 2002 as a small vintage surf shop located on Oahu. Since it's launch, Surf Garage continues to stock anywhere from used boards to high end surfboards from Donald Takayama, Chris Christenson, Bing, Dick Brewer, Hobie, Ryan Lovelace, Joe Beaugess, Jeff McCallum, and many other famous shapers as well as surf goods, and a rental / storage service.

Surf Garage is dedicated to being an active and supportive member of the community. Through partner- ships and programs, Surf Garage continues its commitment to the people and places that surround this beautiful island.
