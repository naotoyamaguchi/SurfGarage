About Us

Surf Garage opened in May of 2002 as a small vintage surf shop located on Oahu. Since it's launch, Surf Garage continues to stock anywhere from used boards to high end surfboards from Donald Takayama, Chris Christenson, Bing, Dick Brewer, Firewire, Hobie, Scott Anderson, Surfboards Makaha, Con Surfboards, and many other famous surfboards as well as surf goods, and a rental / storage service. Surf Garage is dedicated to being an active and supportive member of the community. Through partnerships and programs, Surf Garage continues its commitment to the people and places that surround this beautiful island.
